What Makes This Place Special to me?

This place is special to me since this place will somewhat make you feel that you're in a different era. The feeling is different, the scenary is good that makes me calm. I'm a nature person so I appreciate this kind of place. And just by looking at it, you can guarantee that the air is fresh.

How to Get There

By Subway:

  • Take Line 3 to Gyeongbokgung Station, Exit 5 (3-minute walk).
  • Or take Line 5 to Gwanghwamun Station, Exit 2 (7-minute walk).

By Taxi or Walking:

  • Approximately 6 minutes by taxi from Seoul City Hall.
  • About a 17-minute walk from Seoul City Hall.

Other Palaces in Seoul

  • Changdeokgung Palace: Known for its stunning Secret Garden.
  • Changgyeonggung Palace: A historical site with traditional charm.
  • Deoksugung Palace: Features a unique mix of Korean and Western architectural styles.
