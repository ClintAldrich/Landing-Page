How to Get There

By Plane: Lucerne is well connected via Zurich Airport (ZRH), which has a direct train link to the city. The journey takes approximately 60 minutes.

By Train: Frequent trains run between Lucerne and major Swiss cities like Zurich, Bern, and Basel.

By Car: Lucerne is accessible via the Swiss motorway network, but parking in the city center is limited. Public transport or designated parking areas are recommended.

Top Attractions in Lucerne and Switzerland

In Lucerne:

  • Lake Lucerne: Take a scenic boat tour to explore the stunning surroundings.
  • Mount Rigi & Mount Pilatus: Hike or take a cable car to enjoy breathtaking alpine views.
  • Swiss Transport Museum: Discover Switzerland’s transportation history in an interactive museum.
  • Lion Monument: A famous rock carving honoring Swiss Guards who perished during the French Revolution.

Elsewhere in Switzerland:

  • Zurich: Switzerland’s largest city, known for its vibrant cultural scene.
  • Bern: The Swiss capital, famous for its medieval Old Town.
  • Interlaken: A gateway to outdoor adventures in the Swiss Alps.
  • Geneva: Home to the United Nations and stunning lakeside views.
