What Makes this place special to me?

Shinjuku, Tokyo holds a special place in me because it is renowned as the city where I always dreamed to go. I fantasize about bright lights against the background of busy streets and some hidden beautiful places. I imagine what it will be like walking through its alleys, discovering new adventures, and taking it all in. Even without going there, he has a bit of awe in me.

How To Get There

By Subway:

  • Take the JR Yamanote Line to Shinjuku Station and exit from the East or West gate.
  • Or, take the Tokyo Metro Marunouchi Line or the Toei Subway Oedo Line to Shinjuku Station.

Walking Distance: Kabukicho is just a few minutes away from Shinjuku Station.

Other Sites to See in Tokyo

  • Shibuya Crossing: Experience the famous pedestrian scramble amidst neon lights and towering screens.
  • Akihabara: A haven for gamers and electronics lovers, filled with anime-themed shops and vibrant neon signs.
  • Tokyo Tower or Skytree: Enjoy breathtaking views of Tokyo's skyline at night.
